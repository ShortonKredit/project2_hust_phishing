Trézor staking® | Staking Ethereum (ETH) on TréZor

Staking your cryptocurrencies is an excellent way to earn passive income while supporting blockchain networks. With Trezor, a leading hardware wallet provider, you can securely stake assets like Ethereum (ETH) and Cardano (ADA) directly through the Trezor Suite application.

Key Features and Benefits of Trezor Staking

  • Secure Hardware Wallet Integration: Trezor's hardware wallets, such as the Model T, ensure your private keys remain offline, providing robust security against online threats.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The Trezor Suite offers an intuitive platform for managing and staking your cryptocurrencies, making the process accessible even for beginners.
  • Partnership with Trusted Validators: For Ethereum staking, Trezor collaborates with Everstake, a reputable validator trusted by over 735,000 users across more than 70 blockchain networks. This partnership allows users to participate in staking without the need to run their own validator nodes.
