Welcome to NDAX, your gateway to the world of crypto currency trading.

To start trading, simply log in to your NDAX account. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to access your account securely:

Step 1: Visit the NDAX Website

Open your preferred web browser and go to the official NDAX website by typing "www.ndax.io" in the address bar. Press "Enter" to load the website.

Step 2: Locate the Login Button

Once you're on the NDAX homepage, you'll find the "Login" button at the top right corner of the page. Click on it to proceed.

Step 3: Enter Your Credentials

You will be directed to the login page. Here, you'll need to enter your NDAX credentials:

Email Address: Type in the email address associated with your NDAX account.

Password: Enter your secure password. Make sure it's a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters for enhanced security.

Step 4: Complete the CAPTCHA

As an added layer of security, you may be required to complete a CAPTCHA challenge. Follow the on-screen instructions to prove that you're not a robot.

Step 5: Click "Login"

Once you've entered your email address, password, and successfully completed the CAPTCHA, click the "Login" button.

Step 6: Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For an extra level of protection, NDAX highly recommends enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on your account. If you've set up 2FA, you'll be prompted to enter the authentication code generated by your authenticator app.

Step 7: Access Your Account

Congratulations! You've successfully logged in to your NDAX account. You can now access your crypto currency holdings, trade, deposit, withdraw, and perform various other activities on the platform.

Forgot Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password, don't worry. Simply click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the login process or have questions about your NDAX account, our dedicated customer support team is available to assist you. Visit the "Support" section on the NDAX website for contact information and FAQs.

Your security is important to us, so please ensure you keep your login credentials safe and never share them with anyone. Happy trading on NDAX!
