Empowering people and urban Communities Financially

Hip Hop Economic Development LLC is committed to urban revitalization through innovative real estate, housing, retail, and small business strategies. Founded by Hip Hop Hall of Fame CEO/Executive Producer Sir JT Thompson, this initiative leverages hip hop culture to drive socio-economic growth, emphasizing financial literacy and community economic development, education, and empowerment.
