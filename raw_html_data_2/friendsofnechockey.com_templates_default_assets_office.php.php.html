FRIENDS OF NEC HOCKEY

The alumni game for this season will be played on November 12th. Game at 11 am and reception at Daniel's after, please contact john@lineberrytennis.com to confirm

The Friends of NEC Hockey welcomes you and encourages all individuals who are interested in promoting and assisting the Division III Hockey program at New England College.

By joining the Friends you are showing your support for the team and the coaches.

Over the years, the Friends of NEC Hockey has been instrumental in supporting the NEC hockey program. From a financial standpoint,the monies raised from memberships and fundraisers have allowed us to purchase strength training equipment, video equipment, computers, cameras and large projects (rink renovations, new bleachers, boards and audio systems).


HOCKEY RETURNS! ALUMNI GAME SCHEDULED!

Hockey has returned to the NEC as the Pilgrims kicked off official practices on Monday, October 14th. As they prepare for the regular season, the Pilgrims will travel to Plymouth State on Thursday October 24th for the 1st of 2 exhibition games, faceoff is scheduled for 7:00pm. Please join us at the Lee Clement Arena on Saturday the 26th at 4:00pm for our final pre-season tune-up against MCLA.

MEMBERSHIP DRIVE IS ON!!!

The Friends of NEC Hockey welcomes and encourages all individuals who are interested in promoting and assisting the NCAA Division III Hockey program at New England College to join the Friends of NEC Hockey. The FNEC Hockey relies on the generous support of its' members to continue to support the nationally competitive NEC Hockey program and the Lee Clement Arena. Through the support of its' membership and fundraisers, the FNEC Hockey has been able to provide essential benefits including top quality broadcasts and announcers for all home games, purchased video equipment, computers as well as larger projects including Lee Clement Arena renovations (bleachers, new goals and audio systems).
