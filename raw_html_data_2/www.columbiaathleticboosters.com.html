Who Are We?

Welcome to the Columbia Athletic Boosters! We are a passionate and dedicated group of parents, alumni, staff members, and community members committed to supporting our student athletes and enhancing the overall athletic experience at Columbia Local Schools.  We work with the CLSD athletic director to ease the financial burden of athletic activities (7th – 12th) on the school system and fellow parents.  

We play a crucial role in fostering school spirit, providing financial support, and ensuring student athletes have the resources they need to succeed both on and off the field.

We look forward to your support.
