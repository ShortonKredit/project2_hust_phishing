Are you keen on Dermoscopy?

Then you won’t be able to resist YouDermoscopy: the first app, divided in training levels, that gives you the opportunity to have fun and at the same time improve your ability in the diagnostic identification of dermatoscopic lesions.

“YouDermoscopy” doesn’t replace the typical forms of scientific updating, but it supports them because it is a chance to practice at any time, when you are waiting for a train, a plane or while you are drinking coffee with a colleague.

The App has been developed by Meeter in cooperation with YouDermoscopy, Prof. Giuseppe Argenziano and the Skin Cancer Unit, and through the exclusive contribution of Eau Thermale Avène.

Graphically attractive, easy to use and absolutely innovative!

What are you waiting for?
Download the app now, register and start practicing on more than 700 lesions!
Don’t waste a minute, it’s time to challenge your colleagues and reach the first places in the ranking list!

TRAINING

Get immediately engaged in the 1st level, consisting of 8 game sessions, answer 75% of the cases correctly and pass on to the next levels.
Feel free to get some training: the ranking is anonymous and specifies only your location.

PLAY LIVE

When passing the 3rd level, you will get access to Play Live, an innovative feature through which you can request or provide an opinion in real time regarding a dermoscopic case to your colleagues around the world, who are connected to the app.

BONUS RESEARCH

As part of the world YOUdermoscopy community, with this new feature, you will be able to contribute to scientific research, sharing your experience and knowledge, in a completely anonymous way, on dermoscopy topics, current or to be deepened. Keep training and become part of the dermoscopy of the future!

YouDermoscopy is a project created and developed by Meeter Congressi Srl. This is an Educational game and does not involve app payments or other types of payment. There are no costs related to the creation of a user account and the target audience consists of Medical specialists in Dermatology.
