ABOUT US

We work for you since 1970

For over half a century, our primary objective has been to fulfill the diverse demands of heavy industries in Greece and beyond. We have achieved this by offering a comprehensive range of services.

Throughout the years, our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has driven us to continually innovate and excel in the engineering domain. With a strong focus on precision engineering, technical excellence, and reliability, we have built a reputation as a trusted partner for heavy industries, both domestically and internationally. We take pride in our contributions to the defence sector, supporting the needs of the Greek Army and advancing technological advancements for applications.

WHY NGM

NGM stands out from the competition for several reasons. First and foremost, we prioritize technical excellence, always seeking innovative solutions to meet our clients’ unique needs. Our dedication to quality and meeting delivery deadlines makes us a reliable partner for any project. Additionally, we utilize high-spec CNC machining, enabling us to handle items weighing up to 30 tons. This capability allows us to take on large-scale projects with confidence, delivering exceptional results every time.
