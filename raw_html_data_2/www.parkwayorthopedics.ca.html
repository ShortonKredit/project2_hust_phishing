Welcome to Parkway Orthopedics

At PARKWAY ORTHOPEDICS we specialize in providing customized treatments for all sort of foot problems and disorders. Our team of competent and experienced CHIROPODISTS (foot specialists) are dedicated to ensure your comfort by assessing and evaluating requirements based on and in conjunction with your personal needs (the type of shoes you wear, how much do you walk on a daily basis etc. ). We provide proper attention to each patient so as to cater your needs properly. Your medical condition and respective treatment options are explained to you thoroughly.

PARKWAY ORTHOPEDICS is a leading provider of custom-made foot orthotics. All our products are customized according to patient’s needs. Some of our most popular products are custom-made orthotics, orthopedic and non-orthopedic shoes, compression stockings, body braces and other foot care products.

Most of our Products & Services are covered by Extended Health Insurance Plans. New Patients are welcomed and accepted here!
