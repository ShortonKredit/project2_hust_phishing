Key Features of NDAX®SecurityNDAX® emphasizes security as its top priority, employing industry-leading measures to protect user funds and data. The platform's commitment to safeguarding its users is reflected in its multi-layered security approach:Cold Storage: A significant portion of user assets is stored in offline cold wallets, minimizing the risk of hacks and unauthorized access.Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): NDAX® requires users to enable 2FA, adding an extra layer of protection by requiring a second verification step beyond a username and password.Encryption and SSL: NDAX® ensures that all sensitive data, including personal information and financial transactions, are encrypted. The platform uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology to maintain a secure connection.Regulatory Compliance: NDAX® complies with Canadian regulations, including the Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre of Canada (FINTRAC) guidelines, making sure that the platform adheres to strict anti-money laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) standards.
