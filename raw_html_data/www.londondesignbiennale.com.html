Surface Reflections
5-29 June 2025

Somerset House

Discover today’s designers and ideas that will change our world.

London Design Biennale returns to Somerset House from 5-29 June 2025 for its 5th edition. Featuring world-leading design, innovation, creativity and research by exhibitors from across the globe, London Design Biennale showcases today’s designers and ideas that will change our world.

The three-week international exhibition features curated installations accompanied by a programme of events, thought-leadership talks, performances and workshops.

This year’s theme ‘Surface Reflections’ by Artistic Director Dr Samuel Ross MBE, Founder of A COLD-WALL* and SR_A SR_A, explores how ideas are fuelled by both our internal experiences and external influences. Revelations in life are prompted by personal histories that inform who we are. Together, these form the multifaceted hues of human experience.

Introduction

1m:53s

Artistic Director

Dr. Samuel Ross MBE is the Artistic Director of the 2025 London Design Biennale. The British designer and artist is founder of A-COLD-WALL* and SR_A SR_A, as well as the Black British Artists Grants Programme. Ross has set the theme of ’Surface Reflections’ for 2025, which explores how our ideas are fuelled by both our internal experiences and external influences.

Jury 2025

The London Design Biennale International Advisory Committee and Jury is composed of leading creative experts, with a strong commitment to and appreciation of design. They are responsible for awarding medals to the most significant contributions.

Summit 2024

The third London Design Biennale Summit took place at Chatham House, the Royal Institute of International Affairs on 18 March 2024. Framed by a keynote from 2025 Artistic Director Dr Samuel Ross, the Summit spotlighted the moment we are living in. Panels discussed the major societal and environmental transitions and transformations anticipated in the coming decades, raising the question: "Are We Ready?"
