ASPEN Leads the Science and Practice of Clinical Nutrition

ASPEN is a global interprofessional community of dietitians, nurses, nurse practitioners, pharmacists, physicians, physician assistants, researchers, scientists, students, and other health professionals. We provide resources across every facet of nutrition support, including clinical practice, research, and education.

We envision a world in which every patient receives safe, effective, and high-quality nutrition care.
