BUSINESS DIRECTORY

Centrally located in the Wheatbelt, just 202km east of Perth along Great Eastern Highway, Kellerberrin is a warm, charming community, steeped in a vibrant history dating back from its Indigenous people to European pioneers. The Shire includes the localities of Kellerberrin, Doodlakine, Baandee and North Baandee.

Celebrating its centenary in 2008, Kellerberrin’s longevity is attributed to the passion and commitment of its community, the beauty of its natural attractions and the fostering of new forms of artistic expression.

Kellerberrin is a town where life is as rich as the landscape
