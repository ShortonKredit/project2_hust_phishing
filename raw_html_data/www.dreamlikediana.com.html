Lifes too short to wait on others for what you want to do.

dreamlikediana was created as my open online journal journal to heal my own burn out and chase what actually excites me. By following my curiosity, stepping outside my comfort zone, and refusing to wait around for the right time, Ive built a life that feels mine. I may not have it all figured out, but Ive done things people once told me were impossible.

Throughout these pages you will find: creative inspiration, snowboarding adventures, DIYs, journaling ideas, and all the things that make life more intentional and exciting. Im all about exploring, creating, and finding new ways to make dreams come true.

Most of my success has come from going where everyone else isnt. Because life is too short to wait on others for what you want to do.

So, if youre ready to carve your own path, stick around. Lets make things happen!
