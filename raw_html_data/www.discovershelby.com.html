Special Attractions

Double Oak Park

The newest featured park in North Shelby offers a peaceful escape with hiking and biking trails, picnic areas, playgrounds, and bird watching opportunities. The park caters to different skill levels and offers a diverse range of bird species for enthusiasts.

American Village

Visit American Village in Shelby County, Alabama for an educational and inspiring experience on American history and values without even leaving the state! Founded in 1995, the destination offers historical campus exploration and interactive programs.
